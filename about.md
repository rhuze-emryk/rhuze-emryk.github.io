NeuroFlow Labs is dedicated to making technology more accessible for people with cognitive disabilities, executive function challenges, and neurodivergent thinking styles.

Our content is designed with these principles in mind:
- Clear, concise explanations
- Step-by-step guides with visual support
- Reduced cognitive load through thoughtful organization
- Accessibility-first approach

## Who We Are

We're technology enthusiasts who believe tech should work for everyone, regardless of how their brain processes information. We create guides, references, and reviews that respect different cognitive needs.

## Our Approach

We break down complex topics into manageable chunks, provide visual guides when helpful, and structure information to reduce cognitive load. Our goal is to help you achieve "flow state" with technology, rather than feeling overwhelmed by it.
